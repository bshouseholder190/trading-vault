## Business Overview

**What does the company do?**
Designs and sells GPUs, data center compute (H100/B200), networking (InfiniBand/Spectrum), and the CUDA software ecosystem.

**Competitive moat:**
CUDA software lock-in, supply relationships with TSMC, and dominant share in AI training workloads.
